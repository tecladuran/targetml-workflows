# Documentation

This directory contains reports and analyses describing the different workflows and results obtained within the TargetML project.  
Each subdirectory corresponds to a specific analysis scope or dataset.

---

## [measurements_tarragona](https://github.com/tecladuran/targetml-workflows/tree/main/docs/measurements_tarragona)

Documentation related to the Tarragona measurement campaign.  
This section includes the preprocessing of raw samples and the preparation of the peak table that is subsequently used in downstream analyses.

---

## [targeted_analysis](https://github.com/tecladuran/targetml-workflows/tree/main/docs/targeted_analysis)

Targeted analysis focused on extracting conclusions from the concentrations of selected analytes.  
These analytes are investigated as potential biomarkers for colorectal cancer (CRC).

---

## [untargeted_analysis](https://github.com/tecladuran/targetml-workflows/tree/main/docs/untargeted_analysis)

Untargeted analysis of GC-IMS data without prior assumptions on biomarkers.  
All detected features are considered to explore global patterns, variability, and structure in the data.

---

## [untargeted_22campaign](https://github.com/tecladuran/targetml-workflows/tree/main/docs/untargeted_22campaign)

Untargeted analysis combining GC-IMS data from the 2022 and 2025 measurement campaigns.  
This analysis enables cross-campaign comparison and assessment of consistency and variability across different acquisition periods.

---

## Notes

All reports rely on the same underlying workflows and data organization, ensuring consistency and reproducibility across analyses.
